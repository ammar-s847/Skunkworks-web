Who are we?

The Skunkworks (Team 1246) are a Robotics Team that compete in the FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics Competition. The team is located in Scarborough, Ontario CA and is hosted by the Agincourt Collegiate Institute.
