Who are we?

The Skunkworks (Team 1246) is a Robotics Team that competes in the FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics Competition. The team is located in Scarborough, Ontario CA and is hosted by the Agincourt Collegiate Institute.

Subteams

Mechanical/Design

  • Use CAD software to design Robot Components
  • Use Machines such as Mills, Lathes, and Drill Presses to fabricate mechanical parts
  • Intricately document component design processes

Programming/Control Systems

  • Utilize Standard FRC Electronics and Programming Paradigms
  • Program teleoperation and computer vision
  • Design and wire circuits for electromechanical/pneumatic systems

Business/Media

  • Network with local businesses in order to reach sponsor relationships
  • Photograph team efforts, develop team merchandise, and promote robotics to younger students

Scouting/Drive Team

  • Control the Robot during the game
  • Gain information about other teams in order to create strategies during the competition
